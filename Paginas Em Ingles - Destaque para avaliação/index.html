Why have a cat?

Having a cat is a choice that brings countless benefits. They are independent animals and ideal for smaller spaces, requiring less constant attention. Its presence calms, reducing stress. The relationship between owner and cat is special, providing companionship and joy. Having a cat is a decision that enriches anyone's life.
